Key observations
- VAE and diffusion quality are stable/acceptable for reconstruction + denoising.
- The main bottleneck in Phase-1 is LatentDenseGCN teacher collapse (degenerate AD-only behavior).
- Until teacher quality is fixed, guided generation reliability for class-conditional control remains questionable.

Actionable next fixes (Phase-1 only)
- Strengthen teacher input signal and representation:
  - use richer latent features (mu and logvar or multi-view latent stats) rather than single z snapshot.
- Add stricter teacher objective:
  - optimize by macro-F1 + class recall constraints; not plain CE loss only.
- Improve teacher validation rigor:
  - class-balanced val batches and explicit collapse detector (single-class prediction trigger).
- Add regularization against collapse:
  - entropy floor / label smoothing + calibration-aware early stopping criteria.
